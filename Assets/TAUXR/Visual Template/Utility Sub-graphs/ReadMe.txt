Distance Mask - a general mask around a certain position in space.

Pincher Masks - Outputs a comined mask at certain distance around both pinching points, multiplied by their pinching strength. 

Triplanar Noise - a noise texture with procedural UV's. (For a smooth noise around textured objects)

Viewport Distance Mask - a mask that gives a high value for objects far away from the view. good for giving a saturation decay for far objects. 

ToonShadow - Creates a Sharp Toon shadow around objects by multiplieng their normals with a certain "Sun" vector
